Lunar Gala (LG) is Pittsburgh's largest annual fashion show. Its website features the model lines, members, and ticketing all in line with this year's visual identity: Elysium.

I creatively directed and facilitated cross-functional collaboration in a team of 4 designers and 5 devs. Lead concept design, interaction, and UI | UX for Pittsburgh's largest fashion show's website.

I designed and developed the site's home page GLSL particles to create a library of different particle behaviors to fit with this year's identity, and the UI motion.

● HOME: GLSL Particle system

How can particle motion encapsulate each act?

Act I: Emergence

The dawn of your journey, brimming with hope, passion, and light. Untouched by the world's challenges, yet poised to embrace it all.

Act II: Blossom

In the early steps of your journey, you flourish and grow. You conquer the new horizons, and you can see yourself blooming into the person you have always wanted to be.

Act IV: Embrace

You have walked far, encountering all the pitfalls and flaws of the world, therefore exposing the flaws of you. You have come to realize there is never an end to your journey, but you have come to embrace it.
