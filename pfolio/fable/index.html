Lunar Gala 2026, Fable

Lunar Gala is Carnegie Mellon's student-run fashion show. Each year the creative team translates the year's theme through various modalities like print, web, photo, and motion design on large LED screens as models walk.

I designed several 3D motion pieces, translating the intent of an individual fashion designer's line while maintaining the year's brand.

Explorations
Sticky notes were quotes of their reactions, while star stickers marked elements they preferred.
After reviewing Nate and Frank's designer's line and accompanying narrative, I created a moodboard to define visual elements that best complement their line's message and tone. It helped me understand their taste so I can begin translating their vision.

Since the motion piece would be displayed on a large screen behind fashion models, the piece needed to be slow, abstract, and seemlessly loop to not distract from the fashion lines themselves.
